#### Global PM2.5 Map

This page visualizes the global distribution of average PM2.5 concentration after data cleaning.
Each country polygon is colored according to its average PM2.5 level.

---

#### Map Controls

Use the country selector to focus on one country.
Use the sorting menu to organize countries alphabetically or by PM2.5 level.
Use the quick buttons to preview the top 3 most polluted countries, the bottom 3 cleanest countries, or all six selected countries together.

---

#### How to Read This Page

Darker or warmer colors indicate higher average PM2.5 levels.
Lighter or greener colors indicate lower average PM2.5 levels.
Hover over a country to view its average PM2.5 value.
The map helps connect country-level pollution differences with geographic location.
