#### Purpose

This page summarizes the data-cleaning process before visualization.
It shows how many observations were kept after each cleaning step.
It also lists countries removed during PM2.5 country-level cleaning.

---

#### Cleaning Logic

First, missing or invalid observations are removed.
Then, countries with unreliable PM2.5 records are removed.
Countries with fewer than 3 PM2.5 observations or all-zero PM2.5 values are excluded.

---

#### How to Read This Page

The bar chart shows the number of observations remaining after each cleaning stage.
The removed-country table explains which countries were excluded and why.
This makes the later PM2.5 ranking, profile, and map results more reliable.
