Countries are sorted from highest to lowest average PM2.5.

#### Highlighted Countries

The top 3 countries represent the highest average PM2.5 levels.

#### How to Read This Page

Each bar represents one country.
The bar height shows the country's average PM2.5 concentration.
Darker or warmer colors indicate higher PM2.5 values.
Only the top 3 and bottom 3 countries are labeled to keep the chart readable.
